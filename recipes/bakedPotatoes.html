How to Make Baked Potatoes

A baked potato in the air fryer comes out beautifully crusty on the outside and light and fluffy on the inside!


Ingredients

  • 2 large russet potatoes, crubbed
  • 1 tablespoon peanut oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse sea salt
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Brush potatoes with peanut oil, sprinkle with salt, and place them in the air fryer basket
  3. Cook potatoes until very tender when pierced with a fork, about 1 hour. Serve with sour cream and enjoy!
